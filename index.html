About our gin

Ingredients

Batch 1 is once again made with 100% British wheat spirits and features a botanical selection of juniper berries, cassia bark, coriander seeds, angelica root, pink grapefruit peel, cubeb berries and Darjeeling tea.

Destillation

Our aim is to produce spirits that are accessible in flavour and price, while being of the highest quality of production. In our custom-designed and hand-built Arnold Holstein copper stills, we produce our amazing premium gin.

Packaging

Our bottles are made of the finest quality glass in an old factory 20 miles north of London. The labels are made of recycled high qualiity paper and the bottle is closed with a genuine cork cap.

Featured recipies

Gin & Tonic

  • 6cl gin
  • tonic water
  • 1 lemon slice
  • ice
  1. 1. Put lots of ice in a highballglass.
  2. 2. Fill up with gin and tonic.
  3. 3. Put a lemonslice on top.

Bramble

  • 4cl Gin
  • 4cl Lemonjuice
  • 4cl Simple syrup
  • 2 drips Crème de Mûre
  • Crushed ice
  • Blackberries
  1. 1. Put lots of ice in a glass.
  2. 2. Shake gin, lemonjuice, simple syrup and ice.
  3. 3. Pour into the glass and drip creme de mûre directly in the glass.
  4. 4. Garnish with fresh blackberries.

Watermelon Fizz

  • 2 slices of watermelon
  • 1 limejuice
  • 6cl gin
  • ice
  • sodawater
  1. 1. Put lots of ice in a highballglass.
  2. 2. Mix watermelon and shake with gin, limejuice and ice.
  3. 3. Pour into glass, top with sodawater and decorate with watermelon slices and lime.
